Gender Equity for Policy

Why is the world unequal for women, even as...

  • Women become more educated?
  • More women earn money?
  • More men are supportive of gender equality?
Women consistently earn less, regardless of geography, education level, and occupation

Mothers in the workforce struggle, regardless of age of children and even with a present spouse

Women consistently spend more time in unpaid work globally, even in countries with high education levels and labor force participation
